Map

The following map allows you to explore a visual representation of characteristics of occupied housing units within New York City, focusing on whether these units have adequate plumbing. It draws from US Census Bureau data, organized by tract, that indicates if an occupied building has adequate or inadequate plumbing. As you scroll, it will display counts of housing units as well as counts of units marked with inadquate plumbing.





Data source: US Census Bureau
